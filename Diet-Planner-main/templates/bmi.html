BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It’s used
to assess whether an individual has a healthy body weight for their height.
Here’s how you can calculate your BMI:

Standard Units (US):
Height: Measure your height in feet and inches.
Weight: Measure your weight in pounds.

Use the following formula:
[ BMI = ((weight (lbs) / height (in))^2) \times 703 ]

BMI Categories:
Underweight: BMI < 18.5
Normal weight: 18.5 ≤ BMI < 25
Overweight: 25 ≤ BMI < 30
Obesity: BMI ≥ 30
